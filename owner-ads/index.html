Background
Problem: Vertical SaaS partners (Owner.com first) need a self-serve, embedded ads product for their SMB customers. Restaurant operators are intimidated by Google Ads, agencies are expensive, and a competitor (Pi) is moving fast on the same opportunity.

Approach: A stripped-down skin on the existing Acquire pipeline. Restaurant-tuned defaults, AI-drafted ad copy, plain-English narrative dashboard. Partner funds ad spend (no customer-facing billing surface). Backend services are partner-agnostic from day one so partner #2 onboards via config, not rebuild.

Key differentiator: Every other ads dashboard (Toast, Pi, Google Ads itself) leads with numbers. Owner Ads leads with a paragraph of real English. The numbers are evidence underneath the story.
