Are you currently running, or have you ever run, Google ads for your restaurant? This includes ads run by you, a marketing agency, or anyone else on your behalf.

Why this matters: If two Google Ads campaigns from the same advertiser run at the same time, they'll compete against each other in the auction. Your ad won't perform well, you'll pay more per click, and in some cases Google may suspend the account. We need to be the only campaign running.

Here's what to do

  1. Open Google Ads (ads.google.com) in a new tab and sign in to the account that's running your campaigns.
  2. Go to Campaigns. For each active campaign, click the status toggle to pause it.
  3. Wait until every campaign shows status Paused.
