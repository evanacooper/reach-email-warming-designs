Background
Onboarding redesign: The current Engage onboarding wizard uses a left-only sidebar and orders steps as Brand → Email → Business → SMS → Merge Fields. Business Information is a separate modal that's not strictly required to advance. The redesign moves to a top horizontal stepper with a contextual sidebar (only visible during sub-journeys), promotes Business Info to step 2 as a hard compliance gate, and rebuilds the Email Channel step around an always-provisioned managed domain with optional BYO connection.

BYO sending domain: Tenants currently send from a platform-controlled sending domain. Emails show "via" the platform domain in Gmail/Outlook headers, driving spam/promotions placement and conflicting with Google/Yahoo bulk sender requirements (>5k/day to Gmail). The BYO flow lets tenants connect a branded sending subdomain (e.g. send.acmeinsurance.com) that becomes the DKIM-signing domain for outbound mail. DNS automation via Entri (50+ provider coverage, manual fallback). Multi-domain support so tenants can split marketing and transactional onto different subdomains.

Reference: Klaviyo's branded sending domain flow. Linear ticket ENG-2161 with sub-tasks ENG-2670 through ENG-2749. Entri integration plan reviewed with Matt and Felipe at Entri (May 6 kickoff call).
